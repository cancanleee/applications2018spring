Feature Organization



Home Page:

  • A bright picture (about biking) that occupies the entire page as background
  • logo "QuickCycle" in the middle
  • 3 buttons: "Men" "Women" Kids" at the buttom

Product List Page:

  • This is after clicking any of the 3 buttons on the home page
  • A banner at the top saying Men or Women or Kids
  • Search bar, input field and search icon under the banner
  • Sort:
    • icon with dropdown menu next to search icon
    • relativity
    • sold quantity
    • price from low to high
    • price from high to low
    • rating
  • Filter:
    • a column at the left
    • function
    • feature
    • color
    • brand
    • price
    • rating
  • Search Result:
    • display in grid by default
    • a button that can change between list view and grid view
    • picture of the product with rating at the buttom
    • "add to cart" button will show up on the picture if mouse hovers over the picture
    • product name under the picture as a hyperlink leads to the product page
  • "back to top" button at the buttom

Product Page:

  • 3-column layout
  • product picture at the left
    • a magnifying glass at the lower right corner tozoom in
    • can swith between differnt pictures
  • "add to wishlist" button under the picture
  • brand name at upper middle column
  • product name under brand name
  • some details under product name
  • quantity input box at upper right column; it has a default input of "1"
  • "add to cart" button under quantity input box
  • "add warranty" checkbox under button
  • scroll down to see detailed product description
  • scroll down further to see customer rating and reviews
